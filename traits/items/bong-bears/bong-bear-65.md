# Bong Bear 65

## Visual Properties


**Image Files:**

**Visual Description:** White bear wearing a big cap and an unbuttoned shirt, holding a pizza. The cap is in red to yellow gradient. The shirt is in red, orange, yellow, green, and red again (from top to bottom), with a light pattern of marijuana leaves. The bear is also wearing glasses with a gold frame and bee-shaped sculpture in the middle of it and a golden neck chain with a sign-peace symbol as a charm. The bear is holding a cheesy pizza.
