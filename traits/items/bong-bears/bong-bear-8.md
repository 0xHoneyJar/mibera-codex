# Bong Bear 8

## Visual Properties


**Image Files:**

**Visual Description:** Brown bear with pastel mohawk in a combined color of blue-grayish and pink, and an unbuttoned shirt in the gradient of dark blue, grey, pink, and white (in order from top to bottom). The bear is also wearing a gold chain with a peace sign as the charm; a circle with a line going down in the middle and a pair of shorter diagonal lines on each bottom side. The bear is holding a burger and can be seen having a mouthful of the food in its mouth.
