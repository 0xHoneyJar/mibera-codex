## Visual Properties


**Image Files:**

**Visual Description:** Dark brown bear wearing a buttoned shirt in gradients of red, orange, yellow, and green (from top to bottom), with light patterns of marijuana leaves. The bear is holding a white joint / rollie, and its ears are both pierced; one side with a peace-sign symbol in gold and the other one with two studs and a ring in white.
