# Bong Bear 89

## Visual Properties


**Image Files:**
SS5_bongbear_Bong Bear 89.png

**Visual Description:** Black bear wearing a huge fedora with a wise base and a suit in a similar tone of blue, close to a navy blue. The fedora has a black lining on the inner part, and underneath the suit is a vest in a lighter muted shade of navy blue on top of a white shirt with a black tie. The bear is wearing oval-shaped glasses with a thin frame in white, and is holding a brown cigarette pipe.
