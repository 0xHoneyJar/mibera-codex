## Context


**Cultural Origin:** Virgo, the sixth zodiac sign (August 23 - September 22), is an earth sign ruled by Mercury. Symbolized by the Virgin, it represents precision, practicality, and service.


**Why This Matters:** Mibera visually and astrologically explores all 12 zodiac signs.
