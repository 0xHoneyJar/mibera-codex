## Context


**Cultural Origin:** Aries, the first zodiac sign (March 21 - April 19), is a fire sign ruled by Mars. Symbolized by the Ram, it represents boldness, energy, and initiative. Aries individuals are known for their courageous, enthusiastic, and competitive nature, often taking on leadership roles. Generally, they can be impulsive or headstrong but are dynamic, honest, and thrive on action and challenges.
