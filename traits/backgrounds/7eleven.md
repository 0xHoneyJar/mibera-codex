## Context


**Cultural Origin:** 7-Eleven is a global convenience store chain, originating in the U.S. in 1927 as Tote'm Stores. Known for Slurpee drinks and 24/7 operations in many locations, it has over 80,000 stores worldwide.


**Why This Matters:** Universal waypoint of late-night rave culture — the place you end up at 4am for water, snacks, and cigarettes between sets.
