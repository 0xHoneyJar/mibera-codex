## Context


**Cultural Origin:** Scorpio, the eighth zodiac sign (October 23 - November 21), is a water sign ruled by Pluto and Mars. Symbolized by the Scorpion, it represents intensity, passion, and transformation. Scorpios are known for their determined, secretive, and deeply emotional nature, often possessing strong intuition and loyalty. Generally, they can be secretive or intense but are resourceful and fiercely protective of loved ones.
