# Molecules

*Molecular structure visualizations for Mibera's drug-tarot system. Each image combines archetype, ancestor, and drug.*

---

## Overview

Each Mibera carries a molecule — a molecular structure visualization that embeds the project's drug-tarot system directly into the art. Every molecule maps to a specific drug, archetype, ancestor lineage, and tarot card, linking pharmacological identity to cultural heritage and mystical archetypes.

Molecules contribute to the Swag Score: `total_ss = item_score * shirt_score * molecule_score + hat_score`
