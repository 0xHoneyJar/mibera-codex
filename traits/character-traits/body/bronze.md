## Connections

**Ancestor:** Named for the alloy that defined an age. The Bronze Age (3300-1200 BCE) spans many of Mibera's ancestor civilizations — Hindu, [Mayan](../../../core-lore/ancestors/mayan.md), Greek — representing the first era of widespread metallurgy and trade networks.
